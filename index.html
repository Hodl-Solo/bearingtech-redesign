About

Built on knowledge, not just product

My name is Dwight Williams, owner of BearingTech. I've been involved in the bearing industry for over 30 years, gaining extensive experience in all aspects of this complex and very technical product.

The industry has changed. Sales and distribution are controlled by major suppliers with distributor agreements across the five major manufacturers — creating an environment where they control the market and treat bearings like a commodity, sold at the best price by people with no technical experience.

The industry used to investigate achieved service or DESIGN LIFE then make recommendations to attain the desired outcome. Now this analysis is reserved only for the privileged few — large contracts — or offered to others at a cost. The reason is simple: if they solve the problems, they don't move as much product.

My service to customers is to get back to basics — to share my knowledge and experience to achieve the desired DESIGN LIFE.

⚡

Real example

A conveyor pulley is designed for around 50,000 hours service life. The bearing fails at 10,000 hours.

Major Suppliers: Sell a new bearing.
BearingTech: Sell a new bearing, and investigate why it failed early — provide knowledge, data and procedures to regain DESIGN LIFE. Saving thousands in downtime, labour, lost production, crane hire and more.
